# Editorial Intelligence AI Platform Design System

Source asset: `assets/ee7700a5ec914f83920ec386e5726a4b`

## Visual Direction

- Name: `Quiet Luxury Editorial`
- Theme: `LIGHT`
- Intent: editorial intelligence, restrained luxury, high-end research institution feel

## Core Tokens

- Background: `#fdf8f7`
- Primary ink: `#171411`
- Secondary accent: `#8A5A36`
- Outline: `#cfc5bd`
- Surface container: `#f1edec`
- Card white: `#fffdfc`

## Typography

- Display / headings: `Noto Serif SC`
- Body: `Noto Sans SC`
- Labels: `Work Sans`

## Spacing

- Unit: `4px`
- Gutter: `24px`
- Desktop margin: `64px`
- Max width: `1200px`
- Section stacks: `12px / 24px / 48px / 80px`

## Shape

- Default radius: `4px`
- Editorial card radius: `8px`

## Notes

- Prefer tonal layering over heavy shadows.
- Prefer thin borders over visible card elevation.
- Use bronze accents sparingly for metadata, category labels, and action emphasis.
- Keep Chinese body text left-aligned with generous line-height.
